ID: T1014
Title: Rootkit
URL: https://attack.mitre.org/techniques/T1014
Adversaries may use rootkits to hide the presence of programs, files, network connections, services, drivers, and other system components. Rootkits are programs that hide the existence of malware by intercepting/hooking and modifying operating system API calls that supply system information. [1] Rootkits or rootkit enabling functionality may reside at the user or kernel level in the operating system or lower, to include a hypervisor or System Firmware . [2] Rootkits have been seen for Windows, Linux, and Mac OS X systems. [3] [4] Rootkits that reside or modify boot sectors are known as Bootkit s and specifically target the boot process of the operating system.
